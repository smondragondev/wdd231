Non Profit Membership Level

For non-profit organizations that want to engage with the Chamber community.

Cost:Free

Benefits:

  • Access to networking opportunities with other non-profit and business members.
  • Invitations to attend community and Chamber-hosted events.
  • Basic listing in the Chamber’s online directory.
  • Access to select training and educational workshops.

Bronze Membership Level

For small businesses or startups seeking visibility and growth support.

Cost: Entry-level fee

Benefits:

  • Standard listing in the Chamber’s online directory with business details.
  • Access to member-only networking events.
  • Discounts on selected Chamber-hosted events and training.
  • Quarterly spotlight in Chamber’s newsletter or social media.

Silver Membership Level

For growing businesses looking for stronger visibility and promotional support.

Cost: Mid-tier fee

Benefits:

  • Enhanced business listing with logo and link on the Chamber’s website.
  • Priority access to Chamber-hosted workshops and training sessions.
  • Sponsorship opportunities for events at a discounted rate.
  • Two spotlight promotions (homepage or newsletter) per year.

Gold Membership Level

For established businesses seeking premium visibility and leadership opportunities.

Premium fee

Benefits:

  • Premium homepage spotlight position for maximum visibility.
  • Unlimited event discounts for staff and representatives.
  • Featured advertising in Chamber newsletters, emails, and social media.
  • Opportunity to host or co-sponsor Chamber events and training sessions.
